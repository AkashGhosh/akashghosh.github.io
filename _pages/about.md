### About Me

Hi, I'm Akash Ghosh — a Research Scholar at **IIT Patna**, working on Natural Language Processing, Multimodal AI, AI Safety, and GenAI for Science. I am advised by **Prof. Sriparna Saha** and collaborate with **Prof. Chirag Agarwal** (UVA) and **Prof. Xiuying Chen** (MBZUAI).

I have been a **Visiting Researcher at MBZUAI** and a **Research Intern at Adobe Research**.

My research spans **hallucination in large foundation models**, **multilingual medical reasoning**, **AI robustness and safety**, and **agentic AI for healthcare**. I build systems and benchmarks that push the frontier of reliable and trustworthy AI — especially in high-stakes clinical domains.
